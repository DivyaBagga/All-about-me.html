Smoothie

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Bananas 2
milk 1/4 L

Step 1: Take a blender and plug it in.

Step 2: Add the bananas into the blender.

Step 3: Add the milk into the blender.

Step 4: Blend it and pour into a glass.

Step 5:Enjoy it.

Pesto

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Basil Leaves 2 cups
Garlic 3-4 cloves
Nutritional Yeast 3-4 tbsp
Walnuts 3 tbsp
Lemon Juice 2 tbsp
Salt 1 tsp
Extra virgin olive oil 2-3 tbsp
Water 3-6 tbsp

Step 1: Take a blender and plug it in.

Step 2: Add the ingredients one by one into the blender.

Step 3: Blend it.

Step 4:Enjoy it with pasta, pizza, or whatever you want.

Pancakes

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 5 people
Ingredients Quantity
Pancake flour 2 cups
Milk 200 mL
Homemade Yogurt 4-5 tbsp

Step 1:Mix the ingredients until it is like honey's texture.(Don't over mix this batter)

Step 2: Heat a non-stick pan on low medium and put some oil to grease the pan.

Step 3:Pour a ladel of the pancake batter on to the pan and flip over when the bottom is cooked and is the color gold.

Step 4: Flip over when the other side is cooked and is the color gold.

Step 5:Enjoy it or serve it with syrup, jam or whatever you want.
